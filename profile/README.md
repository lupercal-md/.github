## Lupercal

Lupercal is a digital healthcare platform designed to connect patients, healthcare professionals and clinics through a secure and scalable online environment.

The platform allows clinics to manage professionals, schedules and online appointments, while patients can search for healthcare services and receive remote medical assistance.

Built with a microservices-oriented architecture, Lupercal focuses on scalability, maintainability and clear separation of responsibilities between authentication, clinic management, scheduling and future telemedicine features.
